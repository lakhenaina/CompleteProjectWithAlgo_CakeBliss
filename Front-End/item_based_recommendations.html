📦 ITEM-BASED COLLABORATIVE FILTERING

🔍 How Item-Based Algorithm Works:

  1. Find Products You Liked: The system looks at products you gave high ratings (4-5 stars)
  2. Find Co-Raters: It finds other users who also rated those same products
  3. Find Similar Products: It sees what OTHER products these co-raters also liked
  4. Recommend: If many users who liked Product A also liked Product B, recommend B to you

Simply put: "People who liked what you liked also enjoyed these products."

💡 Item-Based Logic: These recommendations are based on products that are frequently co-rated by users. If other users who rated high on similar products to yours also liked these items, we recommend them!
